Audience

For our audience we are planning to attract businesses that want to become participants in the community. We are also looking to attract those that are have families and that are college aged individuals.

Scenarios

  • A business looking to become a partner in the Chamber of Commerce
  • Important updates in legislation that is needed to be understood
  • What events are coming up for business networking and community outreach
  • What businesses are there in the commumity that is trusted to be in the Chamber of Commerce
  • What activities are there to do in the community.

Personas

Persona A

  • Beginning / seasoned Business owner
  • Middle sized family
  • Experienced with technology related to their skills
  • Looking to develop a long-lasting presence in the community

Persona B

  • Single person in early-mid 20's
  • Not native to the community
  • Working Student / Possible Intern
  • Works hard and enjoys time off from study and work
